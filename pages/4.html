Memes during COVID


With the virus undergoing several mutations , rules and regulations being modified every now and then ,what has not registered any change during this mayhem is the steady flow of memes .In the early onset of the pandemic,with the quarantine norms and other constraints,many of them took solace in memes to keep their disappointments and tensions at bay.The situation remains the same just that the memes have evolved every time a new variant pops up.


They have lightened up our moods and at times triggering a laughter riot with their creative content.What drew people more to them is the fact that they drew heavily on each and everyone of our lives amidst the crisis.There also has been a share of educative and informative ones on how to adapt to the ‘new’ normal.These stand testimonial to the human ability to look out for hilarity in spite of adverse situations running in the background.
